FSSPARM

FSSPARM, PORT1, PORT2
Calculates reflection and transmission properties of a frequency selective surface.

POST1: Special Purpose
Compatible Products: – | – | – | – | Ent PP | – | –

PORT1

Port number of input port. Defaults to 1.

PORT2

Port number of output port. Defaults to 1.

Notes

FSSPARM calculates reflection and transmission coefficients, power reflection and transmission coefficients, and return and insertion losses of a frequency selective surface.
