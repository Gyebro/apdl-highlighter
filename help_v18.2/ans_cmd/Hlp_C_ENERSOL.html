ENERSOL

ENERSOL, NVAR, Item, --, Name
Specifies the total energies to be stored.

POST26: Set Up
Compatible Products: – | Pro | Premium | Enterprise | Ent PP | Ent Solver | –

NVAR

Arbitrary reference number assigned to this variable (2 to NV).

Item

SENE

 — 

Stiffness energy

KENE

 — 

Kinetic energy

AENE

 — 

Artificial energy due to hourglass control/drill stiffness or due to contact stabilization damping

STEN

 — 

Artificial energy due to nonlinear stabilization

--

Unused field.

NAME

A 32-character name identifying the item on printouts and displays. Defaults to a 4-character label formed by the four characters of the Item value.

Menu Paths

This command cannot be accessed from a menu.
