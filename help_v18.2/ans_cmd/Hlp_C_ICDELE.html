ICDELE
Deletes initial conditions at nodes.

SOLUTION: Misc Loads
Compatible Products: – | Pro | Premium | Enterprise | Ent PP | Ent Solver | –

Notes

Deletes all initial conditions previously specified with the IC or ICROTATE command at all nodes.

This command is also valid in PREP7.

Menu Paths

Main Menu>Preprocessor>Loads>Define Loads>Delete>Initial Condit'n
Main Menu>Solution>Define Loads>Delete>Initial Condit'n
