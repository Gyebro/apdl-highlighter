2.7. GUI-Inaccessible Elements

These elements are available via the ET family of commands only and are inaccessible from within the GUI:

REINF263 2-D Smeared Reinforcing
REINF264 3-D Discrete Reinforcing
REINF265 3-D Smeared Reinforcing
SURF251 2-D Radiosity Surface
SURF252 3-D Radiosity Surface
INFIN257 Structural Infinite Solid (generated via the EINFIN command)
