13.175.1. Other Applicable Sections

The CONTA175 description is the same as for CONTA174 - 3-D 8-Node Surface-to-Surface Contact except that it is a one node contact element.

13.175.2. Contact Models

The contact model can be either contact force based (KEYOPT(3) = 0, default) or contact traction based (KEYOPT(3) = 1). For a contact traction based model, ANSYS can determine the area associated with the contact node. For the single point contact case, a unit area will be used which is equivalent to the contact force based model.

13.175.3. Contact Forces

In order to satisfy contact compatibility, forces are developed in a direction normal (n-direction) to the target that will tend to reduce the penetration to an acceptable numerical level.
