13.126. TRANS126 - Electromechanical Transducer

The line electromechanical transducer element, TRANS126, realizes strong coupling between distributed and lumped mechanical and electrostatic systems. For details about its theory see Gyimesi and Ostergaard([249]). This element is especially suitable for the analysis of Micro Electromechanical Systems (MEMS): accelerometers, pressure sensors, micro actuators, gyroscopes, torsional actuators, filters, HF switches, etc.
