13.238.1. Other Applicable Sections

Diffusion in the Mechanical APDL Theory Reference describes the derivation of the diffusion element matrices and load vectors as well as diffusion flux evaluation. If KEYOPT(1) = 1, the damping matrix is diagonalized as described in Lumped Matrices.

13.238.2. Assumptions and Restrictions

A dropped midside node implies that the edge is straight and that the concentration varies linearly along that edge.
